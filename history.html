Breed History

The party yorkie is a special type of yorkie. They are a rare colorway that has a special genetic DNA which makes them special. The parti Yorkie gene is a recessive trait, so the mother and the father must carry the gene to produce a parti yorkie puppy. Parti Yorkies are not mutts and are healthy breeds.
